About Me

Daniel Richmond is a graduate of the Winter 2018 Coding Boot Camp at Northwestern University. In training as a Full Stack Web Developer, Daniel built and deployed applications for the notation of music, the acquisition of Lego sets for unique creations, and augmented reality Capture the Flag.

In his free time, Daniel likes to play with his dogs, read, practice piano, and enjoy the hills of Galena.
